Envoi: Athens, Jerusalem, Mecca: praeparatio coranica

As we have seen from various angles in the chapters of this book, the Eastern Mediterranean witnessed, in the centuries between Jesus and Muhammad, a dramatic transformation of religion: the victory of prophetic monotheism. Together with the abandonment of animal sacrifices, the centrality of prophecy reflects what we can call the religious revolution of late antiquity. The church fathers were no simple bystanding witnesses to these transformations.
